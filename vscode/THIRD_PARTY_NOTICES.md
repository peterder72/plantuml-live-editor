## VS Code PlantUML grammar

The PlantUML TextMate grammar and language configuration are pinned to upstream
commit `7bc1758ed73dc269f5721d78c6c6c01f461d7cb0`. The upstream YAML grammar was
converted to JSON for direct use by VS Code; its tokenization rules are
otherwise unchanged. A copy of the upstream license is also packaged at
